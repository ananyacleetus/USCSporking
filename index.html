Rules:


Objective:
To be the last player standing after all others have been eliminated

Requirements:


    $5 entry fee
  1. To be an upperclassman during the 2014-2015 school year
  2. At least one spork. Players MUST obtain their own. (I will have a few for myself which I may give, but I am not required to.)

How To Play:


Every player will be assigned a target In order for the player to advance in the game, targets must be eliminated HUNTERS MAY ONLY ELIMINATE THE TARGET THEY ARE ASSIGNED (However, the game will eventually become a free-for-all) The hunter must have his or her spork on him to eliminate other people. Targets can only be eliminated if they are not SAFE

Safe:


Holding an odd number of sporks in hand, in mouth, or behind the ear (Sporks held behind the ear must be visible from both front and back. Long hair may partially cover the spork, but the spork MUST be able to be distinguished from a few feet away.) While a teacher is teaching (Anyone interrupting a class will be expelled from the game without refund) From the late bell of a period to the target leaving a room. No sporking in a room while a teacher is present. (Resource center is not safe, Testing center is) NO DISRUPTING CLASSES. During approved activities where the spork can’t be worn (jobs, athletic practice, etc.) If you happen to have lifting for a sport, you MAY remove your spork inbetween sets. HOLDING = Spork behind ear, in mouth, or in hand

Not Safe:


Not holding a spork or holding a spork in a pocket Holding an even number of sporks (EVEN NUMBERS CANCEL EACH OTHER OUT) Holding a broken spork (cracked is fine, broken is not) After >15 minutes have passed since the end of approved activities. Free mods

Sporks:


Must have at least three prongs. Legitimate tines or at least a large distinction between the three parts. It can't just be three nubs. May be any color or material TAPE MAY BE USED FOR AESTHETIC PURPOSES, BUT NOT TO HOLD IT TOGETHER. May NOT be tied/taped in any way. Glasses and other attire may be placed over the spork as long as the spork is visible and wouldn’t fall out without the covering. (If you have to ask, it probably isn’t legal) You may carry extra sporks in your pockets or in backpacks/bags since those place don’t count as being on your body.

Getting Someone Out:


Every hunter will need to track down his/her target If the hunter finds the target and the target is NOT SAFE, the hunter may tap the target with his/her spork and notify the target that he/she is out

Extra Information:


James Finkel is organizing this Winner gets 80% of the pot, 2nd place gets 20% The Pot = $5 x the total number of players Facebook group: USC Sporking 2014-2015 Twitter: @USCSpork Personal Twitter: @Moxxz_ Or, you can message me on Twitter or Facebook Once an interested player has paid, they will be added to a private list. This list, for the sake of competition, will only be known by myself, and in the event of any incident, a committee of randomly selected players. The list will be a spreadsheet that, once everyone has paid, will be randomized. The person above a player will be that player’s hunter, while the person below will be that player’s target. (Any need for clarification of this concept can be shown if need be) Anytime any incident may arise between players, such as players arguing whether or not someone was successfully eliminated, will be handled by myself and two random players not involved in the incident. For the sake of anonymity, I ask that anyone having a problem report directly to me without publicizing the issue. Once each target has been assigned, each hunter will receive a picture and name of their intended target. It is up to the hunter to ascertain more details about their target. For this to work, I request that everyone involved have a picture in the yearbook or one readily available.
